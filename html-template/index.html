Frequently Asked Questions

What Is This?

This is a website with a Baltimore-area DIY events listing with calendar integration. The events are added by automated crawling (with permission) from a number of public websites. The aggregator runs daily-ish and crawls a few different public sites and converts their listings of events into calendar events.

The crawler makes an effort to update when shows are cancelled or moved, but it's not perfect. Often the end times are a guess. Use yr common sense before going to any of these shows, and please be kind in diy and performance spaces.

How Do I Subscribe to This Calendar?

Add this calendar to your phone or computer — it updates automatically. Nothing tracks you, I have no way of sending you ads or anything, it's really just another source for your devices' calendars to pull from. It's a very full calendar, so I recommend turning it on and off as needed; most applications support this:

  • Add to Calendar Works with Apple, KDE, Linux, iCal, and most calendar apps
  • Google Calendar Add to Google Calendar
  • Outlook Personal Outlook account
  • Office 365 Work or school account
  • Download ICS Raw ICS file for manual import

Add the legacy Google Calendar version.

Mobile users note: I had a few reports of the one-click link not working on iPhones. if you click on the above button and it takes you to a weird old google calendar app from 2010, then click "Desktop" at the bottom of the page and you should be able to use it. If it STILL doesn't work... you have to subscribe from a desktop or laptop computer, sorry. Once you do, events will still show up on yr spacephone. I'm not sure why Google is serving calendar software from 2010 and i don't think i can support it, sorry.

Why Aren't You Supporting Google Anymore?

The Boycott, Divestment, and Sanction movement has identified Google as a pressure target. For pressure targets, the movement calls for users to use other services when available. To support this request, the Baltimore DIY Calendar Crawler is now available in many different formats, including raw ics. See https://www.notechforapartheid.com/ and https://bdsmovement.net/Guide-to-BDS-Boycott for more details.

If you still have the old link in your Google calendar, it still works for now, but this may change in the future as the movement's strategies shift. The ics link to Google Calendar above will always work so long as Google supports ics. It should be noted that Microsoft links are also provided, and Microsoft is also a pressure target. I encourage you not to use these services, but they're there if you need them.

Is This a Beta Version?

Yes. There are a few issues to work out - some events with weird formatting don't parse correctly, multi-day events don't work, the de-duplicator needs work with false positives, error handling could be better, and I'd like to automate the crawling so results are accurate up to the hour.
